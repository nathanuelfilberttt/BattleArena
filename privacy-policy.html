Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

War of Meme ("we", "us", "the platform") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the War of Meme platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you provide directly, including:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (which is encrypted and inaccessible to us)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Content you upload (memes, comments, etc.)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We also collect information automatically when you use the platform:

  • Platform usage data (pages visited, time of visit)
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address (for security and analytics)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve platform services
  • Process and display the content you upload
  • Manage your account and provide customer support
  • Send important notifications regarding accounts and services
  • Analyze platform usage to enhance user experience
  • Prevent fraud, misuse, and illegal activities
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may only share information in the following situations:

  • With Your Consent: We will share information if you give explicit consent.
  • For Services: We may share information with third-party service providers who help us operate the platform (hosting, analytics, etc.)
  • Legal Obligation: We may share information if required by law or to protect our rights and security.
  • Public Content: Content you upload publicly (memes, comments) can be viewed by all platform users.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Security measures we implement include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Strict access controls
  • Regular security monitoring
  • Routine security system updates

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze platform usage, and customize content. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect platform functionality.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal information.
  • Restriction: Request restriction of the processing of your information.
  • Portability: Request the transfer of your information to another platform.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your information for certain purposes.

To exercise these rights, please contact us via email: privacy@warofmeme.com

8. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations. When information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

9. Children's Privacy

The War of Meme platform is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will immediately delete that information.

10. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be notified through the platform or email. We advise you to review this policy periodically. Continued use after changes means you accept the new policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@warofmeme.com
  • Discord: War of Meme Official Server
